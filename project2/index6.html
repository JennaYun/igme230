RMSC:

Rochester Museum and Science Center is a museum that represents Rochester's science education.That said, they hold a lot of events about science.

For this exercise, I am redesigning one of their event posters. I focused on creating focal point to catch the audience's eyes. Created in Illustrator.

Target audience : participants

Duration : 1 week

Original design from RMSC

1. No focal point

This poster is overwhelming to me. I don't know where to look with no focal point. Nothing caught my eyes, and it doesn't have any visually attractive points.

2. Lack of spacing

Since it has too much information, it doesn't have enought white space and negative space.

3. Color

The main title and numbers have color, but this doesn't take advantage of using color.
