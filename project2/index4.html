*All works were created in Cinema 4D.

Wine Advertisement

The goal of this project is to understad 3D basics by modeling a simple object that we can see in daily life. We added a tagline to make it look like a real advertisement. Reflections on the wine bottle and glass allowed me have an appropriate perspective about lighting.

Corner

For this project, we were supposed to model a room or corner and tell a story that represents a specific period and location. I picked Japan in edo period. To generate peaceful feeling, I added volumetric lighting and I could add the beam of light from the window. Also, I was focusing on the texture to make it look realistic.

Practical project - Tube

This practical project required students to create an unique label of a product that has tubular shape. I chose a sun block product because we can easily find and use it. I tried to emphasize the reflections on the curves.
